About-Us

our shop, Hanuman Sweets, is well-known for its delicious and freshly prepared sweets. Earlier, the shop was known as Sri Lakshmi Narayana Sweet Stall, which was established in 2004. The business was founded by Srinu Babu Moturi, with Durga Rao as the co-owner. Over the years, our commitment to quality and taste has made our sweets popular among customers, and we continue to serve the same authentic flavours with pride.

We are the family of sri hanuman sweets
